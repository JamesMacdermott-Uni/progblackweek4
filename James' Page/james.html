Positive Impacts of Computers: Computers in Education

Created by James Macdermott. Highlighting the factors making computers in education so important

Access To Education and Infomation using Computers

⚪Internet Access

Computers are the primary means of internet access. Internet access has allowed the sharing of knowledge on top of vast repositories of infomation and resources such as courses, journals and libraries.

⚪Online Courses

Ability to gain qualifications and knowledge, usually obtained within a physical place of education such as a school, now obtainable from your home.

⚪E-books and E-readers

Physical, and perhaps expensive or exclusive, books can now be accessed using the internet and there is less need for carrying many books when several can be accessed by even small computers such as phones. Devices such as phone also allow for the same portability of education and infomation that a book would hold.
